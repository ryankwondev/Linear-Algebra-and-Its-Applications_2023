## 1.2. The Geometry of Linear Equations

- Row(행, 가로), Column(열, 세로)
- Row Pictur (가로로 찢기)
- Column Picture (세로로 찢기)

### Row Picture
$$2x-y=1, x+y=5$$

### Column Picture
$$ x \left[
\begin{matrix}
    2 \\
    1 \\
\end{matrix}
\right] + y \left[
\begin{matrix}
    -1 \\
    1 \\
\end{matrix}
\right]  = \left[
\begin{matrix}
    1 \\
    5 \\
\end{matrix}
\right] $$
